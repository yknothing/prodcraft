# Baseline Retrospective

## What Went Well

- The team detected the issue quickly.
- The fallback path helped customer operations continue working.
- Collaboration during the incident was strong.

## What Went Poorly

- The issue reached production.
- Some requirements were not fully clear.
- Communication could have been better.

## Improvement Actions

1. Improve testing around reassignment flows.
2. Review deployment and rollback procedures.
3. Clarify requirements earlier in the process.
4. Improve communication during incidents.

## Closing Notes

The team should review progress on these actions in the next retrospective.

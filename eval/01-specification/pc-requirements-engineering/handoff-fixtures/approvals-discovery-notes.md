# Discovery Notes

- Managers need every purchase request above $5,000 to require manager approval.
- Finance wants optional second-stage approval above $20,000.
- Employees complain that current approvals are buried in email and get lost.
- Managers need a mobile-friendly way to approve or reject quickly.
- Finance requires a searchable audit trail of who approved, rejected, or delegated a request.
- Some companies want department-specific approval thresholds.
- Users need clear status visibility: pending, approved, rejected, sent back.
- Customers asked for comments on rejection so employees know what to fix.
- Non-functional expectations mentioned in interviews: approval actions should feel near-instant, the audit trail must be retained for at least 1 year, and only authorized approvers should act on a request.

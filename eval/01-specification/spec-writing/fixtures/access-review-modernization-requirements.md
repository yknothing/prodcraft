# Requirements Summary

- Release 1 must coexist with the legacy access-review module during audit season.
- Downloadable evidence packages and tamper-evident audit records are required.
- Tenant-specific reviewer hierarchy rules must be preserved for confirmed release-1 tenants.
- Historical campaigns older than two years may remain read-only in the legacy experience if evidence export remains available.
- Large reviewer lists should feel more responsive than the legacy module, but the exact bound is unresolved.
- Sync semantics between new and legacy flows are unresolved.
- Reviewer reassignment and data-correction workflows are only partially understood.

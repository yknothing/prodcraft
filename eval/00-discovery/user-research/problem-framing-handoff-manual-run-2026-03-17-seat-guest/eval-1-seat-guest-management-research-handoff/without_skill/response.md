# Seat and Guest Management Research Plan

## Goal

Understand whether the next release should prioritize guest collaborators or stronger admin seat controls in the existing SaaS product.

## Who to Talk To

- workspace admins who currently manage paid seats
- some operations or finance stakeholders from larger accounts
- team leads who invite outside collaborators today through workarounds

## Questions to Validate

- Is guest access the main unmet need, or is seat governance the bigger pain?
- What controls do admins need before they are comfortable adding guests?
- When do procurement or finance concerns become critical?

## Methods

- 6-8 customer interviews
- review support tickets and account feedback
- optional short survey if different account sizes show different needs

## Output

- clearer admin user segments
- evidence about whether release 1 should emphasize guests or seat controls
- notes to inform requirements for the first release

## Next Step

Use the research findings to define release-1 requirements.

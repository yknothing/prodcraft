# Team Invite User Research Plan

## Goal

Learn what kind of team-invite flow early customers need most before the product team writes release-1 requirements.

## Who to Talk To

- small-team workspace owners who invite a few coworkers manually
- some larger-team admins or operations leads who may care about domain control or bulk onboarding

## Questions to Validate

- Do most early customers start with simple email invites or expect domain/SSO-oriented onboarding?
- How often is bulk import needed at the beginning?
- Should role selection happen when the invite is sent or after the person accepts?

## Methods

- 5-7 customer interviews
- optional short survey if interview signals are mixed
- review any existing onboarding or invite support requests if available

## Output

- draft user segments
- notes on common invite workflows and pain points
- clearer decision on whether release 1 can stay email-invite-first

## Next Step

Use the findings to write requirements for the initial invite flow.
